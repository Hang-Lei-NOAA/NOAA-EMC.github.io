4 ! SUBPROGRAM: NGMFLD COMPUTES LAYER MEAN NGM FIELDS
5 ! PRGRMMR: TREADON ORG: W/NP2 DATE: 92-12-22
6 !
7 ! ABSTRACT:
8 ! THIS ROUTINE COMPUTES A HANDFUL OF NGM LAYER MEAN
9 ! FIELDS. THIS IS DONE TO PROVIDE A FULLY COMPLETE
10 ! ETA NGM LOOK-ALIKE OUTPUT FILE. THE SIGMA (LAYER)
11 ! FIELDS COMPUTED BY THIS ROUTINE ARE TABULATED BELOW.
12 !
13 ! SIGMA (LAYER) FIELD(S)
14 ! --------------- --------------
15 ! 0.47191-1.00000 RH
16 ! 0.47171-0.96470 RH
17 ! 0.18019-0.47191 RH
18 ! 0.84368-0.98230 RH
19 ! 0.85000-1.00000 MCONV
20 ! WHERE
21 ! RH = RELATIVE HUMIDITY
22 ! MCONV = MOISTURE CONVERGENCE
23 !
24 ! LAYER MEANS ARE A SUMMATION OVER ETA LAYERS MAPPING INTO
25 ! THE PRESSURE RANGE CORRESPONDING TO THE SIGMA RANGE ABOVE.
26 ! THE CALCULATION OF THESE BOUNDING PRESSURES IS DONE AT
27 ! EACH HORIZONTAL GRID POINT BASED ON THE SURFACE PRESSURE.
28 ! EACH TERM IN THE SUMMATION IS WEIGHTED BY THE THICKNESS OF
29 ! THE ETA LAYER. THE FINAL LAYER MEAN IS THIS SUM NORMALIZED
30 ! BY THE TOTAL DEPTH OF THE LAYER.
31 
32 !
33 !
34 ! PROGRAM HISTORY LOG:
35 ! 92-12-22 RUSS TREADON
36 ! 93-07-27 RUSS TREADON - MODIFIED SUMMATION LIMITS FROM
37 ! 0.66*PSFC TO 0.75*PSFC AND 0.33*PSFC
38 ! TO 0.50*PSFC, WHERE PSFC IS THE
39 ! SURFACES PRESSURE. THE REASON FOR
40 ! THIS CHANGE WAS RECOGNITION THAT IN
41 ! THE LFM 0.33 AND 0.66 WERE MEASURED
42 ! FROM THE SURFACE TO THE TROPOPAUSE,
43 ! NOT THE TOP OF THE MODEL.
44 ! 93-09-13 RUSS TREADON - RH CALCULATIONS WERE MADE INTERNAL

46 ! 98-06-16 T BLACK - CONVERSION FROM 1-D TO 2-D
47 ! 98-08-18 MIKE BALDWIN - COMPUTE RH OVER ICE
