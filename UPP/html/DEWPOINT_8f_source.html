4 ! SUBPROGRAM: DEWPOINT COMPUTES DEWPOINTS FROM VAPOR PRESSURE
5 ! PRGMMR: J TUCCILLO ORG: W/NP2 DATE: 90-05-19
6 !
7 ! ABSTRACT: COMPUTES THE DEWPOINTS FOR THE N VALUES
8 ! OF VAPOR PRESSURE IN ARRAY VP.
9 ! .
10 ! . THE FORMULA
11 ! .
12 ! . VP = 0.611 * (X**A) * EXP( (A+B)*(1-X) )
13 ! .
14 ! IS USED TO GET DEWPOINT TEMPERATURE T, WHERE
15 ! .
16 ! X = T3/T, T3=TRIPLE PT TEMPERATURE,
17 ! VP=VAPOR PRESSURE IN CBS, 0.611=VP AT T3,
18 ! A=(SPEC. HT. OF WATER-CSUBP OF VAPOR)/GAS CONST OF VAPOR
19 ! . AND
20 ! B=LATENT HEAT AT T3/(GAS CONST OF VAPOR TIMES T3).
21 ! .
22 ! ON THE FIRST CALL, A TABLE TDP IS CONSTRUCTED GIVING
23 ! DEWPOINT AS A FUNCTION OF VAPOR PRESSURE.
24 ! .
25 ! VALUES OF VP LESS THAN THE FIRST TABLE ENTRY
26 ! (RVP1 IN THE CODE) WILL BE GIVEN DEWPOINTS FOR
27 ! THAT BEGINNING VALUE. SIMILARLY , VP VALUES THAT
28 ! EXCEED THE MAXIMUM TABLE VALUE (RVP2 IN THE CODE)
29 ! WILL BE ASSIGNED DEWPOINTS FOR THAT MAXIMUM VALUE.
30 ! .
31 ! THE VALUES 0.02 AND 8.0 FOR RVP1 AND RVP2 YIELD
32 ! DEWPOINTS OF 233.6K AND 314.7K,RESPECTIVELY.
33 ! .
34 !
